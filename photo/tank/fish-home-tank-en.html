A cave for fish in my aquarium

On the photo you see a part of my 200 liter tank with big plastic cave for fish . The cave is covered by little stones of gravel. Plants grow in special trays on the cave. The cave is quite attractive for fish. Catfish and cichlids all day long spend in the cave. Even barbuses hide there sometimes.
