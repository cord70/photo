Auratus

( Melanochromis auratus )

Auratus is aggressive, especially to its relatives, but also to the fish that are of the same size or the same color as auratuses. Almost not suitable for common tank. To get auratuses fry in the aquarium there should be holes, bushy plants, caves, where the females may spawn, care their fry, and hide themselves.
