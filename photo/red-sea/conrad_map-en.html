Conrad resort, satellite view

This view is from google-maps. You can see that immediately beyond the fence the desert begins. More russian resort Baron is on the left hand from Conrad ( Coral Sea Sensatori Resort ). Baron is also green and well arranged inside its fence. Earlier the desert was everywhere on Sinai.

Conrad is rather English and Italian resort. Who isn't interested with animation (like me) may love Conrad because it has free gim and there are a lot of silent zones where night noisy entertainments do not sound. There is a shop alley between Baron and Conrad. Here you can buy a mask or souivenires.

Both hotels have got long shore with beautiful corals. Swimming with mask there I saw octopus, squid, scat, lionfish, anemonfish , dolphin. Sometimes water was so calm that you could photograph a lionfish by a usual camera through surface of water. (However normally it's windy there.)
