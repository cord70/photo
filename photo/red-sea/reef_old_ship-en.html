Coral reef between Sinai and Tyrant island

Coral reef is a hill on sea bottom, which reaches sea surface, that is dangerous for ships. Because of rare rains, lack of rivers and still sea the water is always transparent here. Transparent water is seen at bottom side of the picture. At top side you see an old Soviet? tanker. The photo was taken near Tyrant island. This old ship is well seen through a binocular from hotels Conrad and Baron .
