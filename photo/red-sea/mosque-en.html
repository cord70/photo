A mosque at Naama-bay, Sinay, Egypt


Sun rises from behind
the Tyran island

Near the road from airport to Naama-bay tourists see a mosque on the background of Sinay's mountains. The mosque stands quite far away from houses. It makes the view more attractive. I often saw this mosque because the resorts where I stayed ( Conrad, Concodre, Lavita) are near the airport. Near the airport sun rises from the sea and sets behind mountains.
