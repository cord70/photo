Burrfish

( Cyclichthys spilostylus )

When the fish is afraid of you or to scare you it is puffed up and the thorns are arise. Normally the thorns are clasped to skin. Thorns are placed in white spots on the back and on black spots on the stomach. Due to the thorns the fish feels safe and you may approach closely to it.
