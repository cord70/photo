Masked puffer

( Arothron diadematus )

Grey puffer fish with black fins, black spots, black mask. This fish has got strong teeth (beak) for crushing corals, like teeth of a Parrot fish. The fish has got 4 teeth. When it bites corals you may clearly hear crispy sound.
