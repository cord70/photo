Picasso Trigger Fish

( Rhinecanthus assasi )


Open trigger

A fish with a yellow mouth, big orange eyes, a vertical black wedge at eyes, three horizontal dark lines near the tail. Color of back is straw-grey, stomach is white. On the head these colors are divided by a black wedge strip above the mouth. There are also blue and black hat of strips at the head. It is a Picasso.

Picasso is a triggerfish . Its dorsal fin (trigger) is not seen on the picture because it is closed now. The fin may open to hold the fish in a hole of a coral.
