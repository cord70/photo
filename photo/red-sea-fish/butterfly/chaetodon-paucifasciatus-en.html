Crown butterflyfish

( Chaetodon paucifasciatus )

The fish body is whitish with dark vertical chevron-like stripes. Back parts of the tail and the dorsal fin are of red-orange color, but the most back rims of them are white. The fish swim by couple or by small groups. Live in Red sea only. Size 10-14cm is appropriate for a home saltwater aquarium.
