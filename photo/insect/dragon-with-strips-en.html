A dragonfly on a grass


On a dry branch

The wind swings grass. It is easier to photograph a dragonfly on a rigid branchlet. Under the bright sun a dragonfly may sit on a branch very long. I don't know if it is asleep or maybe it is watching me this time. Once I watched a dragonfly an hour or longer. It wasn't immovable. It turned against wind and sun, rambling on the top of the branchlet.
