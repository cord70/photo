A home centipede


( Santa 500x400 )
( Sherlock 600x480 )

( Scutigera coleoptrata )

Photos were taken in an hotel room at 6th floor. Quite long an patiently I was chasing the bug, so it couldn't hide in a hole and got tired. Finally it agreed for a short photo session. After that the centipede still run away from me.

House centipede may bite. The sting resembles bee's one. But it is a useful animal - it may hunt for home insects such as cockroaches and bed bugs.
