A mosquito on fabric


Macro with a p/s camera

The photo is made by Nikon CP880, which minimal macro distance is 4 cm. Maybe I also used an additional 4x loupe to increase the object. You see a transparent mosquito's abdomen, fine anatomy of legs and antennae. Blood fills only half of mosquito's abdomen. Mosquito's antennae have hair. The mosquito sits on the net of window tulle.

The "Slim mosquito" is photographed through a loupe 7x by old digicam HP C-200 in 2000. That camera had minimal macro distance 15cm, and it was focusing very slowly.
