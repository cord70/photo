Fly's legs

A fly with red eyes on a reddish background. The fly's body colouring is like a chess white-black squares. Its legs are hairy and have got cloven hoofs. The photo was taken when I test my new camera Nikon cp880 in Sokolniky park near Moscow.

Two moths in a dancing pose were noticed near the fly with cloven hoofs. Such Blue butterfly is common in Moscow region. And a bee-like Syrphida on a dandelion is from a South region of Russia.
